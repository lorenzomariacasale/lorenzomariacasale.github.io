## About Me

Hi, I'm Lorenzo Maria Casale. Welcome to my website! 

Proudly born and raised in Rome, I hold both my Bachelor and my Master of Science in Economics from Tor Vergata University of Rome, specializing in Econometrics.

I'm now a graduate student in economics at the University of Zurich. I joined the graduate program in September 2022, after a 2-year pre-doc program in the same department with UBS Center professor, Prof. Hans-Joachim Voth, and with Prof. Bruno Caprettini. 

I'm mainly interested in the areas of economic history, cultural economics, and political economy.
